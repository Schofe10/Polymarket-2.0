App Notes
Read this before trading.
This app reads public market data and ranks candidates. It does not place trades. Bucket opportunities depend on correct grouping. Arbitrage opportunities can disappear once spreads, fees, and slippage are considered. Use this as a screening tool, then verify manually.
How this app scores markets
Beginner version
1) It fetches live Polymarket markets and their YES/NO token IDs.

2) It reads midpoint and spread for each side.

3) It computes four checks:
• YES + NO under $1
• grouped bucket totals under $1
• liquidity quality from spreads
• your model edge = fair probability minus market probability

4) It ranks candidates and lets you save the best ones.
